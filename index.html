💡 Getting the best results

Speak in full sentences — "I will send the contract by Friday" is detected as an action; "contract Friday" is not.

State commitments clearly — phrases like "I'll follow up", "can you send me", "we agreed to" all trigger action detection.

Mention urgency — words like "urgent", "ASAP", "by tomorrow" automatically flag items as High priority.

After recording, review in the triage screen — approve what matters, reject what doesn't with one click.

No transcript? Just type bullet-point notes in the box — even rough notes are enough for action detection.
